India



Brief Description and Geography

India is a country in South Asia with a diverse population,
rich culture, and a variety of landscapes India is the world's
seventh-largest country by area, covering 3,287,263 sq km. It's
bordered by the Indian Ocean to the south, the Arabian Sea to the
west, and the Bay of Bengal to the east. India's terrain includes the
Himalayas in the north, the Thar Desert in the west, the Ganges Plain in
the north, and tropical rainforests in the south. India is the world's most
populous country, with a population of over 1.39 billion people. It's home to
thousands of ethnic groups and hundreds of languages. It's captital is New Dehli.
