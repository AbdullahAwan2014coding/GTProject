History of the U.S

The U.S all began when British decided to settle to the "new land". British
was competing with the other European countries for land and power. Each
country wanted the most. So Great Britan decided to settle in America.
America's first settle ment was Jamestown, Virginia. The settlement
didn't work out well, and ended in war between the settlers and the
natives.
