History of the U.S

The U.S all began when British decided to settle to the "new land". British was competing with the other European
countries for land and power. Each country wanted the most. So Great Britan decided to settle in America. America's
first settle ment was Jamestown, Virginia. The settlement didn't work out well, and ended in war between the settlers
and the natives. Then, more and more people came to America, and they were unhappy about the way the British were
treating them. In fact, the revolutionary war had many causes, such as taxes, no taxtaion with out representation,
and some act, like the Boston Tea Party and the Boston Massacre. People were extremely unhappy abot the way the British
were treating them. They started telling people they should break of from Great Britan! That was what started the
Revolutionary War, which ended in U.S victory at the battle of Yorktown, where they celebrated by playing Yankee
Doodle, the song the British used to mock americans! Now that's karma! The U.S signed the Treaty of Paris, which
stated that the U.S would become it's own country, and that it would expand towards the Mississipi River. Now,
you're probaly wondering, how did the U.S get bigger then? Well, the Louisiana Purchase is how! The US wanted to
buy New Oreland, so they could have access to the Port of New Oreland. Now, the problem was, France owned the land
at the time, but they managed to strike a deal. The US said we would buy New Oreland for $10 million! France, who
had just been in a war, needed the money, and wanted to go home, said they would give them all of their land for
$15 million dollers. The US said yes, and France had enough money to pay off their debts and go home, while the US
doubled in size!
